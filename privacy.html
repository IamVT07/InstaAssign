Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us at InstaAssign

1. Information Collection

InstaAssign collects personal information such as name, phone number, email, and academic details when you use our services. We use this information solely to provide our services and communicate with you about your orders. This may include your class, subject, academic level, and specific requirements for your assignments.

2. Data Protection

Your personal information is kept confidential and secure. We do not share your data with third parties without your consent, except as required by law. All data is stored securely and protected from unauthorized access. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information from theft, loss, alteration, and unauthorized access.

3. WhatsApp Communication

We primarily communicate through WhatsApp for order confirmations, updates, and customer support. Your WhatsApp number is used only for service-related communication and is kept confidential. We will never share your WhatsApp number with third parties or use it for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

Our website may use local storage and cookies for the following purposes: storing your theme preference (light/dark mode), maintaining your browsing session, and improving user experience. These do not store sensitive personal information and can be cleared from your browser settings at any time.

Please contact us at instaassign01@gmail.com or via WhatsApp to exercise these rights.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites and services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies separately.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for educational purposes for students of all ages. If you are under 18, we recommend having parental consent before using our services. We do not knowingly collect information from children without parental consent.
