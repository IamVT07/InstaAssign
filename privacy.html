Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Your privacy is important to us at InstaAssign

1. Information Collection

InstaAssign collects personal and professional information necessary to deliver our services effectively:

  • For Academic Services: We collect your name, phone number, email, class, subject, academic level, and specific assignment files or requirements.
  • For Business Digital Solutions: We may collect company/organization names, professional contact details, brand assets, temporary project credentials, website URLs, and specific technical requirements.

We use this information solely to provide, maintain, and optimize our services, fulfill your project specifications, and communicate with you regarding your ongoing orders.

2. Data Protection

Your personal and professional data is kept confidential and secure. We do not share your information or custom project files with third parties without your explicit consent, except as strictly required by law. All data is stored securely and protected from unauthorized access using industry-standard digital security measures to safeguard against loss, theft, alteration, and breaches.

3. WhatsApp Communication

We primarily communicate through WhatsApp for order confirmations, live progress updates, and instant customer support. Your WhatsApp number is used only for service-related communications and is kept completely confidential. We will never share your WhatsApp number with third parties or use it for automated marketing broadcasts without your explicit consent.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Our website utilizes local storage, cookies, and standard analytical tracking tools (such as Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics). These are used to store your theme preferences (light/dark mode), maintain active browsing sessions, monitor platform health, and evaluate generalized traffic patterns to improve our digital offerings. These tools aggregate anonymized data and do not store sensitive personal information. You can easily manage, clear, or block cookies via your browser's settings at any time.

You have the right to:

Please contact us at instaassign01@gmail.com or via WhatsApp to exercise these rights immediately.

Our website may contain links to external sites, hosting platforms, or external utilities. We are not responsible for the structural data handling or privacy practices of these external sites. We highly recommend reviewing their independent privacy policies separately when navigating outside our platform.

InstaAssign structures operations across two target segments:
